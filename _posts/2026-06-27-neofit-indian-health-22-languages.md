Open almost any fitness app and it assumes two things: you eat like the West, and you read English fluently. For most of India, both assumptions are wrong. NeoFit is my answer.

## What it is

[**NeoFit**](https://naveenneog.github.io/NeoFit/) — *"Sehat, simple banayi"* — is a **science-informed Indian health and fitness app**:

- calorie and macro tracking that actually knows **Indian food** — *dosa, biryani, gulab jamun*
- **on-device food recognition** and **AI-generated food photos**
- **AI exercise videos** and guided workouts
- **Health Connect** sync, offline-first
- fully localized into **22 Indian languages**
- **powered by Azure AI**, built in **Kotlin + Jetpack Compose**

## The good

Health advice only works if you can understand it and see your own food in it. By speaking **22 languages** and knowing the Indian plate, NeoFit meets more than a billion people where they actually are. **Inclusion is a health outcome** — and that's #AI4Good.
